1. Money&Sense Missions

Advisory Mission in Ethic Investments

We currently live in a world where financial systems are really important and relate to almost every individuals and organization. Money is in the center of every organized human action. Thus, saving money is necessary to plan the future for individuals as for organization. Association without lucrative goal are a particular kind of organization since they do not focus on profits but on general interests. Nevertheless, they need money to sustain. Unfortunately, saving money at the bank might sometimes lead their savings to finance projects that are in contradiction with the association's mission of general interests. That is why we created "Money&Sense". Our consultancy team work with associations to establish a strategy to invest safely their money in projects that are in line with their own mission. We want to offer associations without lucrative goal the possibility to be fully consistent with their overall missions, even in the way they manage their money.

Illlustration

For example, we could help an environmental association to find innovative green projects in which to invest their savings. We try to ensure the maximal level of safety and returns (even if priority is given to the ethic of investments) in our investments advice.

Advisory Mission in Financing Activities

We also intervene as consultants in order to help associations establishing a financing strategy and advise them on the activities they can implement to finance themselves: how to find sponsors/benefactors or donators, what kind of activities to raise funds, etc...

2. Money&Sense Inspiration

The concept of our firm is born from our interest in ethical and solidarity-based finance. The Finansol association, which is an ambassador of this engaged, useful way of making finance was a great source of inspiration for the development of our project.
